About Bobbel

Our Bobbel-bottle is the first all-in-one bottle for sparkling water. You cannot just carry your water with you but it also allows you to make sparkling water on the go with a simple push on the button.

Easy Installation

Just turn in the CO2-cartridge and you are ready to go!

One cartridge will provide you with up to 4 liters of sparkling water.

Fizz It

Make your own sparkling water during your trip and enjoy!
